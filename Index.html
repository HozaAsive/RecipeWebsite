Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz (225g) spaghetti
  • 4 slices of pancetta or guanciale
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup (100g) grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water and cook the spaghetti until al dente. Drain, reserving 1/2 cup of pasta cooking water.
  2. In a skillet, cook the pancetta or guanciale until crispy. Remove from heat and drain on paper towels.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, grated Pecorino Romano cheese, minced garlic, and a generous amount of black pepper.
  4. Combine the drained pasta, cooked pancetta, and egg mixture in a large bowl. Toss well, adding reserved pasta cooking water as needed to create a creamy sauce.
  5. Serve immediately with an extra sprinkle of Pecorino Romano cheese and black pepper.
